What We Do

IVC Amateur Radio Club is committed to serving the community via spreading the word of Amateur Radio

STEM Oriented

We explore the intersection of Amateur Radio and STEM, through which we enrich our knowledge in fields such as electronic engineering and computer science.

On the Air

It is in the spirit of Amateur Radio to be on the air. We support community members of every age by providing licensing resources leading to active operators on the air.

Always Prepared

We are Amateur Radio operators. We are prepared to support our community with our technical skills and provide backup communication When All Else Fails.

Amateur Radio Testing

As a service to the community, we provide free Amateur radio examination on a monthly and on-demand basis.

Please find our currently scheduled sessions on HamStudy. If no session is scheduled at the moment or you would like an alternative date or time, feel free to reach out to us via Discord or email. We will gladly schedule you an online session at your convenience.

The date and time for our upcoming sessions are also available on our club calendar
